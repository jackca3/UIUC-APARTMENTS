# Competitive Landscape

| Competitor | What They Do | Strength | Weakness |
|------------|-------------|----------|----------|
| Zillow | Large apartment and housing listing platform | Huge database of listings | Not student-specific and reviews are not very detailed |
| Apartments.com | Listing marketplace with professional apartment search tools | Strong filters and polished listings | Feels biased toward landlords and lacks real student experience |
| Google Reviews | Public review source tied to apartment locations | Easy to access and often has a lot of reviews | Users are unverified and review quality is inconsistent |
| Reddit (UIUC threads) | Informal student discussion and apartment opinions | Honest student perspectives | Disorganized, hard to search, and not structured for apartment comparison |
| RateMyDorm / similar platforms | Student-focused housing review platforms | More relevant to student audiences | Limited coverage and often outdated |

## Our Differentiation
Apt.ly is more targeted than general listing platforms because it is built specifically for UIUC students and restricted to verified `@illinois.edu` users. That gives it a better chance of combining trust, structure, and relevance in a way that broader platforms and informal discussion boards do not.
