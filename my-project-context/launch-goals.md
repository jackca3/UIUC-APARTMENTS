# Launch Goals

## Target Users
The first users are friends, classmates, members of business organizations, and other UIUC students who are actively apartment hunting.

## Timeline
Soft launch within 1 to 2 weeks, followed by a broader campus push before peak leasing season.

## Success Metric
The main success metric is the number of verified reviews submitted, with an initial goal of 50 reviews.

## Definition of "Launched"
Apt.ly is considered launched when real UIUC students are actively using the platform and the team has collected the first 50 verified apartment reviews.

## Open Questions
- Which student groups or channels will drive the strongest early adoption?
- How quickly can apartment coverage expand to meet user expectations?
