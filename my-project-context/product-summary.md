# Product Summary

## What It Does
Apt.ly is a UIUC apartment review platform that lets verified UIUC students browse apartments, save favorites, and write honest reviews about things like noise, management, and overall quality. The goal is to make apartment hunting less guesswork-based and give students access to more useful on-the-ground information before they sign a lease.

## Who It Is For
Apt.ly is for UIUC students searching for housing who want real, unfiltered information from other students instead of relying only on landlord marketing or generic listing sites.

## Current Status
The product is live and deployed at `https://uiuc-apartments-two.vercel.app/`.

## The One-Liner
Apt.ly helps UIUC students find better housing by giving them verified, student-written apartment reviews.
