# Apt.ly Social Content Pipeline

## Deliverables
- `audience-analysis.md` - target audience, brand voice, and content pillars
- `7-day-calendar.md` - seven days of posts across LinkedIn, Instagram, and X
- `index.html` - visual content hub for reviewing and sharing the calendar

## Deployed Content Hub
Live URL: `https://jackca3.github.io/aptly-content-hub/`

## Purpose
This content pipeline turns Apt.ly's product positioning into a repeatable launch-week marketing system. It is designed to help the team build awareness among UIUC students, create consistent messaging, and share a polished campaign asset in presentations or launch materials.
