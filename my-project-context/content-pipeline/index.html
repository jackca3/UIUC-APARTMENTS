Apt.ly content pipeline

Launch-week content for a campus-specific housing product.

This hub turns Apt.ly's value proposition into a week of platform-specific content built around trust, apartment-hunting pain points, and a clear student-first call to action.

Audience

  • First-time off-campus leasers
  • Students comparing multiple apartments quickly
  • Campus connectors who share useful tools

Message

  • Apartment hunting should not depend on scattered rumors.
  • Verified student reviews make housing decisions less risky.
  • Apt.ly is built for UIUC, not generic real estate traffic.

Day 1 - Monday

The housing problem

LinkedIn

Apartment hunting near UIUC is weirdly high-stakes for something built on scattered Reddit threads, landlord listings, and word-of-mouth. We built Apt.ly to give students verified, student-written apartment reviews so housing decisions feel more informed and less like a gamble. If you are tired of guessing, we are building this for you.
