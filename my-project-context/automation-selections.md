# Automation Selections

## Category A - Marketing & Acquisition
**Selected:** Social Content Pipeline
**Why:** Apt.ly needs awareness among UIUC students, and a repeatable social content system is a practical way to build visibility before leasing season without relying on paid marketing.

**Why:** Apt.ly already has useful but messy feedback from testing, and turning that into clear themes, priorities, and fixes will help the product improve before a broader launch.
